Sign Up Ulta Beauty Account


Create an account on Ulta Beauty portal to win Rewards. For registering an account,


  1. Go to official Ulta Beauty website.
  2. Tap on the 'Sign In' tab.
  3. On the upcoming screen, tap on 'Create an Account' under new user.
  4. Now, put in the details like First name, Last name, Email ID, Password, Mobile no., DOB, State and other.
  5. Then, hit the 'Continue' button.
  6. Click the 'Agree' button to the Terms and Conditions.



Apply for Ulta Credit Card

To apply for Ulta Credit Card, all you need to do is visit the official Ulta Credit Card website and have following-


  • Belong to Majority Age group in State/ Territory.
  • Possess authentic Photo-ID.
  • Have SSN/ SIN.
  • Have mailing address.

With the above details ready with you,

  1. Simply open your web browser, type ulta.com/credit.
  2. Login your Ulta Credit card account.
  3. Now, go ahead with the on-screen instructions to apply for Ulta Credit Card.
  4. Put in the Necessary details.
  5. Then, you will get notified on approval of your Credit Card.
  6. Lastly, within 3 business days of Credit card application, you will receive the Welcome kit.

For customers who have applied for online Ulta Credit Card, enjoy discount on their First purchase.


Ways to Make Payments

Mainly there are three ways to make payment through Ulta Credit Card-

Ulta Online Payment

  1. Visit to Ulta Beauty website.
  2. Tap here the 'Manage your acount' section.
  3. Then, tap on 'Sign In' button for Ulta Credit Card Payment login.
  4. Now, click on the 'Pay Now' button, enter amount and other details of Payment.
  5. Proceed ahead with Payment steps.
  6. Fill in your details asked-
    • Credit card Account No.
    • ZIP Code
    • Identification type
    • Last 4-digits of SSN
  7. Now, complete on-screen instructions to manage payments. Fees is not charged for paying online.

Make Payment Via Mail

Send payments to - Ulta Beauty Rewards Credit Card PO Box 650964 Dallas TX 75265-0964. This may not charge you any platform fees.


Ulta Credit Card Payment by Using Phone

Make a Call at the Comenity Capital Bank Contact Number to proceed ahead with Payment. Making ulta credit card payment on Call may charges platform fee.

NOTE:

  • Remember to make payment at Ulta Beauty store you need to foillow any of the above Payment mode. No other way is valid to manage payments at Ulta beauty store.

Check Your Balance

For checking your Ulta Credit Card Balance, you can simply

  1. Visit to Ulta Beauty Credit Card website.
  2. Login your Ulta Account.
  3. Then, tap on the 'Manage Your Account' tab.
  4. Now, Perform Ulta Credit Card login.
  5. You will get full details of Ulta credit card.

Alternatively, you can contact Comenity Capital official portal to know your Ulta credit card balance.


How to use Ulta Gift Card online?

For using Ulta Gift Card online, you simply put in the Ulta beauty Gift Card Number and PIN. You will find PIN at back side of Ulta Gift Card.
For redeeming your Ulta GIft card, select the Products you wish to use. Then add them to Shopping cart. During the checkout process, tap on the "Gift Card" tab, put in your PIN & Gift card number.
Complete details mentioned and tap the 'Apply' button. Now, the amount gets deducted from total amount of your Gift card. This involves other details like Credit card details too.
